The Story

Quinton Brogan is a singer-songwriter creating music that blends heartfelt storytelling with raw, authentic sound.

With tracks like "Billy The Kid," "Person I'll Become," and "Parachute" resonating with a growing audience, Quinton's music captures the honest, lived-in moments that make a song feel like home.
